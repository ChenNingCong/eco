R-oko
no model
How to play R-oko: Play cards from your hand onto a factory's recycling side (matching color). If the recycling value reaches 4+, you claim the top factory token and the recycling side clears. Otherwise, 1 + recycling value cards are drawn to the waste side. You pick up all waste cards from that factory. Hand limit = 5; excess goes to your penalty pile (-1 pt each).
Scoring: Factory tokens only score if you have 2+ of that color. No penalty = bonus. Click "New Game" to start!
